About This Calculator

This free online calculator handles everyday arithmetic - addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, percentages, and sign changes - right in your browser. Switch to Scientific mode for trigonometry (sin, cos, tan), square roots, powers, logarithms, factorials, and constants like π and e. Every calculation is saved to a history log you can revisit or clear at any time. No installation, no sign-up, and no data ever leaves your device. It works on desktop and mobile, and supports full keyboard input for fast entry.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this calculator free to use?
Yes, completely free with no sign-up required.

Does this calculator have scientific functions?
Yes, toggle to Scientific mode for sin, cos, tan, square root, powers, logarithms, factorials, and more.

Does this calculator keep a history of my calculations?
Yes, recent calculations appear in the History panel below the calculator. Click any entry to reuse its result, or use the Clear button to reset it.

Does this calculator work on mobile?
Yes, it's fully responsive and also supports keyboard input on desktop.

Does my calculation data get sent anywhere?
No, everything runs locally in your browser.
